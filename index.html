My Favourite Hobby is Dancing


My hobby is dancing.I like Bollywood dance.I am 11 years old and I read in 5th standard.After my online dance class finishes our teacher sends the dance video.I practice the dance when I get free time.Before this pandemic I had to learn Folk dance and Classical dances I did'nt like that much but when this COVID-19 pandemic started, my regular dance classes stopped. One day I received a mail about E-Shiddha bollywood dance workshop. I thought that I will join the workshop.I felt a little nervous but I made many new friends from different states, one is from Mumbai, one is from Pune, one is from Odisha,one is from Bangalore and one is from Hyderabad. Our teachers name is Moushumi Shaha.She is born in Kolkata,but lives in Idore. Now I am learning bollywood dance from her and one student and his mother is learning dance from her.They are from Delhi. Dancing is not only enjoyment but also good for our mind and body. It keeps us physicaly and mentaly fit.
